AWRL : Asian Workshop on Reinforcement Learning

About

Reinforcement learning (wikipedia) enables agents to learn how to best interact with their environements, resulting in intelligent behaviors of agents, and is a central research topic in artificial intelligence.

AWRL aims at boosting the developement of reinforcement learning from foundamental research to industrial applications. While sitting in Asia-Pacific Rim, AWRL intends to be an exciting platform for researchers and practitioners worldwide for exchanging idea, sharing insights, and initializing cooprations, in the domain of reinforcement learning. We invite reinforcement learning researchers and practitioners to participate in this world-class gathering
